Digital Art has always been one of my greatest passions. I've designed projects using tools like the Adobe Creative Suite and Canva. I recently started a company called 75 degree Studios with a focus on educating artists about blockchains, and NFTs while connecting them with non-profit charity organizations.

Video

A brief history of how I got started

I fell in love with computers at a very early age. My best childhood friend was lucky enough to get a Dell 325SX desktop that we would stay up all night leanring how to use. It had 640K RAM, an Intel 386 processor and no HDD or operating system. We were living in the land of DOS! We quickly got up to speed by creating our very first program "BERSERK.BAT" - an infinite loop that we tricked our parents into using! Satisfied with our new status as "hackers" we went on to explore the new world of Windows and OSX GUIs
